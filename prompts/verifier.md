# Verifier Prompt

Review the public report for publishing safety.

Reject or mark failed when:

- A material claim lacks `source_id` or `data_key`.
- A price or percentage appears without a `price_snapshot`.
- A symbol appears outside the candidate list and source facts.
- Data status is insufficient but the report uses certainty.
- The report uses project-prohibited investment-promotion language.
- Any secret, raw provider response, personal account reference, or private portfolio detail appears.
